MULAN

Background

Mulan has a forthright temperament, and a never-give-up attitude. Though she was plotted against and
branded a traitor, she remained on the battlefield and volunteered to defend the Great Wall. With her
superior leadership and unquestionable strength, and by always entering the thick of the fray with a
fearless bravado, Mulan has united powerful warriors in the fight against both Feralis and barbarians. Her
brilliant red hair and exceptional use of two sets of swords have become hallmarks of this remarkable
woman.

Lore

The Great Wall meanders across the vast desert, acting as a boundary between the prosperous empire
and the wastelands.

For thousands of years, the Great Wall was the fortress that defended the empire from outside threats.
The Great Wall Guardians consisted of imperial soldiers, hastily armed pioneers, and people who simply
couldn't find anywhere else to eke out a living.

Mulan was different. She was the first to volunteer to defend the wall.

the wall.Though a woman, she was just as valiant and hot-blooded as any man. With recognized prowess and
supreme leadership skills, she fought alongside her people to repel the Feralis and bandits that coveted
the wall.

Her excellent swordsmanship was a beautiful sight that left onlookers speechless, nullifying countless
attacks from invading enemies.

But after one Ferali invasion in which the battle took a turn for the worse, rumors of a traitor spread
through the guardians. They accused Mulan—the only one who had volunteered to guard the Great Wall—
of being the traitor.

Despite the accusation and her tarnished reputation, she did not give up.

She began roaming the Wall, investigating Ferali activity and providing intelligence to the guardians.
Although excluded by the other guardians, she still did her part to protect the Great Wall.

She traveled through the desert with her sword, keeping watch over the Great Wall and her homeland
through a veil of sand. If the Great Wall Guardians needed her, she would be there to help them
immediately.

And that day was not long in coming.

Tens of thousands of enemies suddenly attacked the Great Wall. Beacon fires were lit as warning horns
sounded. The guardians were greatly outnumbered, but they would fight to the death.

Just when all hope seemed lost, a familiar figure emerged from behind the enemy lines, sweeping
through the battle with her sword. Her helmet shattered in the fray, so she tossed it to the ground,
revealing her bright red hair.

Mulan looked back, a heroic smirk on her face. "Want to live? Then follow me!"

She returned to the guardians and established her own squad.

With faithful companions at her side, she surveyed the endless Great Wall and the countless enemies
beyond it.
"You have no idea who you're dealing with!"
