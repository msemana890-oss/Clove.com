WUYAN

Background

As an orphaned hybrid, Wuyan survived the Southlands because she knew that power and money were
the only ways out. Savage and determined, she stuck to her way of doing things even at Jixia Academy.
From stealing tomes to working as a mercenary, no one could predict what her next wild step would be.
Only when she shattered the chains that bound the Feralis with her hammer did it finally dawn on people
that this formidable warrior had become an unstoppable force.

Lore

Born in the Southlands, Wuyan was discriminated against at a young age because she was half human,
half ferali. Her innate power surfaced when she turned nine years old, and that was enough for her to
fight back against those who bullied her.

When she got older, she wandered the land and eventually reached Jixia, then enrolled as a student at
Jixia Academy. Eccentric, aloof, and arrogant, even Fuzi had a hard time communicating with her.

She firmly believed that both power and gold were required for survival, so she occasionally joined the
troops in Yong to earn her mercenary's purse.

Repulsed by the shackling of the Ferali troops, she shattered the chains with her hammer. The sovereign
of Yong, Zheng, and his generals had been trying to remove the barrier between the races, so they
appreciated Wuyan's actions.

Then, Wuyan, who shattered expectations wherever she went, fell in love with a man. Their union made
her part of the imperial family, who were very reluctant to accept her into the fold.

Now a noble, Wuyan quickly donated the land of Jixia to Fuzi. Perhaps in some way, Jixia was like a home
to her.

Like everything in her life, the stability and warmth of her married life did not endure for long. Her
husband was killed in a Ferali ambush, and the loneliness that she had buried so deeply in her heart
suddenly resurfaced. With nothing left for her in Yong, she returned to Jixia and began investigating the
Feralis and their secrets. She wanted to know what happened to her beloved, to be informed of her
ancestry, and to understand her powers.

Those who might seek to stop her would be wise to ponder if they can withstand a pounding from her
colossal hammer.

"Try me! You won't know what hit you."
