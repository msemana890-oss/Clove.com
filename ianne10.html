FUZI

Background

When Fuzi founded Jixia Academy, he became the first of the Three Sages of Jixia, the other two being
Zhuangzi and Mozi. Fuzi strongly believes that any attempt to seek wisdom or explore sorcery beyond the
proper boundaries of one's knowledge will only lead to ruin. To impart knowledge in a relatively safe and
controlled environment, Fuzi accepts any capable student, and teaches them according to each
individual's aptitude. As a result, Fuzi's name has become like a great lantern to which the talented
people of the land are drawn.

Lore

"Without a great sage, this land would be doomed to darkness!"

Everyone in Primaera knows this saying. Even a three-year-old will tell you that the strongest person in
the world is Fuzi. This is a legend, but also an unshakable belief.

Nobody knows how old the man is. They only know that, like a vast and rushing river, he has always been
around.

Nor do they know how great his power is. They only know that, like a towering snowy mountain, the peak
cannot be glimpsed.

There was once a sage named Jiang Ziya, whose power was comparable to that of Fuzi. At one time, they
had been close companions.

Jiang Ziya had inherited the knowledge of antiquity and passed it down to his followers in the form of
both mechanical arts and sorcery.

Fuzi, meanwhile, foresaw the potential dangers of this approach: The pursuit of the supreme, the root of
knowledge of heaven and earth, was quite likely to cause students to lose all sense of fear, and thus stray
from the path of life. Indeed, it was just such an unrestrained exploration of sorcery that caused so much
destruction in ancient times, and which led to the creation of the feralis that posed such a grave threat to
humanity and ultimately caused King Zhou's downfall.

Unwilling to accept Fuzi's advice, Jiang Ziya was bent on recreating the glory of antiquity. This
disagreement eventually led to a complete severing of ties between the two great sages.

Later rulers agreed with Fuzi's view. In order to pass on valuable knowledge without bringing calamity
upon the world, Fuzi founded an academy in Jixia, becoming the first of the "Three Sages of Jixia",
alongside Zhuangzi and Mozi. Jixia Academy attracted the most gifted pupils from across the continent,
teaching each according to their own talents and never treating any student differently based on their
place of birth or clan affiliation. Over time, Jixia Academy became the epitome of higher learning in
Primaera, a holy land for talented youths.

The young martial artists regarded it as an honor to be instructed by the founder of Jixia Academy
himself. Fuzi was an erudite sage in their eyes, yet simultaneously a goofy and approachable old pal.

As time went on, students trained by Fuzi at Jixia Academy spread across the continent, and although
they belonged to different factions, they were steadfast pillars of strength in the world. As Fuzi's fame
continues to resonate throughout Primaera, his martial arts and beliefs continue to be passed down, and
the flame of knowledge shall never be snuffed out.

"I'm just here to teach. And save the world while I'm at it!"
