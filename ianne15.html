LU BU

Background

If the original Lu Bu was a nearly invincible fighter, the reborn Lu Bu is a nightmare unleashed upon the
world. But after the mighty warrior vowed his unending love to Diaochan, the deadly dancer, he was
slowly entangled in her web of schemes and lies, and ultimately met a tragic end. Unwilling to accept
death, he chose to return as a fiend. Using his Crescent Halberd to capture the souls of his enemies, Lu
Bu has been consumed by the hatred in his heart, and the fiend's wrath shall set the world ablaze.

Lore

Lu Bu managed to get away from the enemies that had surrounded him, but his injuries were too serious,
and he fell on the side of the road, gasping for breath. His wounds attracted flies, but the odious insects
never got to devour their enormous prey. A wandering caravan stopped, and gentle hands delivered water
to his lips. Her hands were soft. "Live." Her voice was a whisper from heaven. That one word triggered his
survival instinct. He fought death, and wrested his life from its grasp.

Night fell. The grass swayed in the wind as the girl danced by the fire. The man teetering on the edge of
death was spellbound. Was he dreaming? Dreams, fleeting dreams. How many years had passed since
then? He had superhuman strength, and was undefeated. But when people talked about the Warlord,
they spoke of his recklessness and lack of strategy. Lu Bu scoffed quietly to himself. They either wanted
to use him, or they wanted him dead. A cruel world indeed. Some may be willing to become a weapon for
others, like... Bai Qi. But Lu Bu was not interested in such a fate. "I will stay alive, and obtain everything I
want." He had endured it all silently through betrayal after betrayal. Just a little more... He had thought he
would have it all.

Until the woman named Diaochan danced before him. He had seen a dream in her movements—a long
time ago, by a bonfire far away. That flash of joy had been beautiful, like the spring of life... a memory he'd
revisited over and over. And he woke up, once again. His body fell, but his mind was clear. He opened up
his heart to her, but it ended in his tragic downfall. It was the end for the hero.

Xu Fu laughed raspily. "You have a choice, you know?" What choice? Live on, and be punished through
reincarnation? Live because no one wants to die, though everyone wanted you dead. "There's a great
secret of this world hidden in the Heavenly Scrolls," said Xu Fu. "I've been trying to uncover it, and you're
my best bet." "If you don't want to give in to reincarnation, then surpass it."

Somehow, he regained movement. He touched something familiar... his Crescent Halberd. His strength
was coming back, surging through his body. His consciousness flickered like a candle in the wind,
dimming and flaring, but his determination burned bright. If he couldn't become a human, he would
become a fiend. Live, and then... "I am the world's worst nightmare!"
