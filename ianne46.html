FLOWBORN

Background

According to future historians, Jixia Academy was where it all began. But if we turn back the clock to the
present—at the unnoticed gates of Jixia Academy—a student has just returned from his studies abroad...

The wheels of fate have begun to turn, and no one can break the cycle already set in motion. You knew
you'd end up walking that thorny road, because it was never ice-cold rationality and planning that guided
you, but the warmth that arose from within in the moment while fighting alongside your companions.

Lore

Upon your return to Jixia after studying abroad, some inexplicable things began happening to you. It
started with magic surging through you and accidentally destroying the dorm's tea table, taking an entire
month of living expenses with it. After that, when Luban's mechcraft parts fell from above, your body
moved on instinct. In a flash, the bracelet on your wrist transformed into a shield as you charged forward
to block the fall, destroying the windows of the teaching building in the process.

Incidents like these were too numerous to count. At the slightest thought, different weapons would
emerge from your bracelet, and the moment they landed in your hands, your body would instinctively
respond with perfect precision. Both sorcery and martial arts just came naturally to you, as if imprinted
within you, despite your certainty that you'd never trained in any of it. You didn't know where the
weapons came from, nor where you'd learned these moves that seemed to land you in constant trouble.
But despite how unfamiliar it all felt... there was something oddly familiar about it too.

Even in Jixia Academy, where talent abounds, this was far from common. Not to mention you have no
idea how this all came to be. Your enrolment into Jixia Academy remains clear in your memories, even
Fuzi's friendly suggestion that led to you studying abroad... But what happened after that? Racking your
brains for answers only ends with splitting headaches.

Headmaster Fuzi seems to know something, yet refuses to say much. It makes you feel uneasy. But for
the sake of the truth, you decide to go along with Fuzi's advice and find your own truth in battle.
