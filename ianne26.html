FEYD

Background

Feyd has become a household name in Jade City for a number of reasons: his profligate spending, heir to
the throne, and the infamous lack of fixed employment as beneficiary of the public purse. His subjects
likely don't realize the same happy-go-lucky prince is, in fact, one of his city's most tireless defenders on
a never-ending quest to uncover its darkest secrets. Naturally, he ignores all such rumors out of his
preoccupation with the fight against injustice. Indeed, a look over the city walls at night might just reveal
the unrestrained figure of a dashing prince.

Lore

Legend has it that many cities have risen throughout the history of Alsahraa, yet few ever manage to
overcome the conflicts and chaos that ensue. Jade City is one of the exceptions, and it prospers due to
the jade trade. Feyd, the eldest prince of Jade City, has become a household name in the city for a
number of reasons: his profligate spending, heir to the throne, and the infamous lack of fixed employment
as beneficiary of the public purse. Being a king or a hero is never his desire. The only certain facts are
Feyd's dedication to staying rich and idle, and his love for jade. Beautiful jade is a common pursuit among
the people of Jade City, and as one known as the Jaded Son from birth and the possessor of the ability to
control Soul Jade, Feyd is no exception. He never gets tired of sending his jade craftsmen out searching
and carving jade into various masterpieces for his collection, or to be given away as gifts.

According to the people of Jade City, he is a dashing young man with no regard for money; but to the
officials, he is a free-spirited prince who does as he pleases. Yet his younger brother Saeed finds it
impossible to believe that the outstanding older brother he grew up with has been reduced to such an
idle critter. Love and hate toward Feyd coexist in Saeed's heart.

Yet no one knows that at the age of 14, Feyd, whose sole wish was to continue his extravagant and
carefree princely life, witnessed his father's miserable death at the hands of General Roya. Once Roya
took control of the city, as the older brother, Feyd could do nothing more than hide his hatred deep within
while playing the role of the useless prince. Nor would anyone know that it was the only reason Feyd
could avoid Roya's cunning gaze, move in the shadows, and help the senior officials escape death right
under Roya's nose. Feyd even managed to secretly plan out and execute the rescue of his younger
brother Saeed, who'd been held at the Great Wall since the incident in the market. After years of planning
and subterfuge, when his younger brother's plans were discovered by Roya during an evilly planned
feast, Feyd just went ahead and took vengeance with his own hands. It was Garo and the others who
helped him escape danger after getting severely injured during the ordeal. Yet, during Saeed’s long-
desired Hero's Eve, in order to protect his younger brother and Jade City, Feyd was forced to once again
put an end to the schemes in a way Saeed couldn't accept. Over the years, he's been hiding many a truth
deep within. Happy-go-lucky and frivolous on the outside, yet secretly protecting everything he loves
while endlessly searching for the secrets lurking beneath Jade City's bustling appearance.

Although various discussions frequently find their way into his ears, the prejudice and jokes spreading
throughout Jade City have never bothered him, nor can he be bothered to become someone
acknowledged by others. Indeed, a look over the city walls at night might just reveal the unrestrained
figure of a dashing prince.
