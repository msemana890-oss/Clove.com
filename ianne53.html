LADYSUN

Background

To the noble Lady Sun, "powdering one's nose" means to have one's face covered in gunpowder fumes after
annihilating those who bully the weak. Following in the footsteps of her elder brother, Sun Ce,
Lady Sun grew up with the desire to make life better for all of Jiang. One day, after fighting back
against a sudden pirate attack, word of her prowess with the cannon spread far and wide. Be it helping
her brother reform the country or repelling enemies, this bold lady is always willing to go all out.

Lore

The affluence of the land of Wu often drew the greedy gaze of pirates and pillagers, and even the ronin
of distant Kyokutou. After establishing Jiang, Sun Ce sent his right-hand-man, Zhou Yu—known as the
"Ironwilled"—to put an end to those marauders. As one of the most gifted of the imperial family,
Lady Sun asked to accompany him and begin her martial arts training.

Before long, word of the over-energetic princess and her specially modified cannon spread far along the
coast. Sometimes she was the high-and-mighty "pirate princess", sometimes the righteous and
upstanding "enforcer of discipline", and yet other times the punisher of evil and promoter of good, the
"lady thief"...

Rumors swirled about a mysterious girl with incredible destructive power. Whenever anything bad
happened, people would make bets on what type of image she would take on. Even her usually cool-
headed guardian, Zhou Yu, was exasperated by her endless mischief, and could only laugh in resignation.

During her journeys, Lady Sun befriended Xiao Qiao, the banished daughter of the Qiao family. They had
much in common, including their penchant for romance novels and, of course, their fondness for boys.
These happy days did not last long, however. Lady Sun soon learned that her older brother, Sun Ce, had
been assassinated.

When Zhou Yu tried to convince her to take the throne, she flatly refused: "What do I want with the
throne? That is of no interest to me! What I care about is making the land of Wu into a better place—and
I'm gonna use gunpowder to make it happen!" And so, Lady Sun hauled her cannon from place to place,
blazing her own path toward the future.

Along the way, she eventually found clues that Sun Ce was still alive! Thus, the young woman set forth
resolutely to track down and rescue her dear older brother, and to find out the truth.

"Get outta my way! Now!"
