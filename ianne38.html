YAO

Background

As a young admirer of Li Bai, Yao has always been filled with passion and ambition. He seeks the carefree
life of a wandering swordsman that his idol practices to perfection. This aspiration prompted Yao to join
Jixia Academy and found the Team of Stars, which would let him take part in Zhuangzi's Dream Trial.
During this endeavor, he made new friends, gained strength, and learned much about himself. Although
his team won the competition, this was just the beginning of Yao's journey...

Lore

An impassioned youth, Yao always dreamed of becoming a great hero. From a young age, he would
always compete with his older sister, who was much more cool-headed and quite a bit stronger. Despite
their differing temperaments, both children ended up studying at Jixia Academy, driven by the hope of
expanding their horizons at Primaera's most venerable institution.

Yao made many friends as he was signing up for Zhuangzi's Dream Trial. He and the Team of Stars he
captained overcame every challenge in their way, achieving great feats as they did so. In one Dream Trial
match, Yao discovered the very foundation of his awareness and, in so doing, mastered the Star Power.
He swung his sword with newfound strength, and like a single star glimmering in the tapestry of the
heavens, Yao finally shone with a light that was all his own.

He may have finally defeated his older sister in the Dream Trial, but his journey as a hero was just
beginning.
