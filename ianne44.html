ARLI

Background

Arli is a dancer at Changle District, known throughout Chang'an for her graceful movements. Her renown
provides an excellent cover, allowing her to gather information while performing at various banquets
reserved for the rich and famous. As she twirls the parasol deftly between her fingers, she continues to
safeguard the justice of Chang'an from behind the scenes.

Lore

Arli considers herself to be a lucky girl.

She was chosen out of over one hundred children and taken to Changle District in Chang'an. She relished
the opportunity to change her fate and worked hard at learning to dance until eventually she became a
famous dancer in Chang'an City known for her graceful and elegant style.

Arli became used to seeing the cold cruelty of the world from a young age, but she never let that taint her
kind heart and believes that everyone has the right to follow their dreams. She wants to maintain justice
in Chang'an and bring good fortune to those less fortune. Because of this, she joined the mysterious
Yaotian Society to explore the secrets of Chang'an.

When curfew begins and a banquet for dignitaries reaches its climax, Arli begins her mission disguised as
a dancer and sneaks into the banquet to secretly gather intel. Ruthless documents, important files, and
memos containing the fate of many civilians are being enacted by tyrannical oppressors, greedy scum,
and cold-blooded nobility. These people should have their crimes exposed, their true colors revealed, and
be punished for their inappropriate behavior. This would give innocent people the chance to be happy.
This is the secret to justice hidden in the shadows.

Arli has never slipped up and has the help of her Yaotian Society partners. There was only one time when
she worried that she was about to be exposed.

She was about to leave a banquet with a document that she obtained when a downtrodden man grabbed
her by the sleeve and said he'd pay her a large sum for a dance.

At first, she was disgusted, but then she noticed the pain in his eyes. He probably wanted to throw away
the money he'd earned with his blood, sweat, and tears on doing something crazy.

She put on a haughty look and said, "It's not that easy to get a dance from me. You'll have to make a bet
with me first. If you can guess three things about me, I'll dance for you. If I guess three things about you,
you have to walk me home."

In the end, the guy lost the bet and took Arli back to the hall.

She already knew all his stories: a hidden attack, an official declared a traitor, a plea for help ignored, and
a desire for justice to be restored.

She thought about his misfortune, and invited him, who called himself Pei, to join the society.

With permission from the leader, Pei became a member of Yaotian.

Pei, who hailed from the Great Wall, brought back sad memories of the Wall for Arli.

A long time ago, she'd parted from someone she admired, but she'd not been able to find the courage to
speak up and express her feelings, so she just secretly watched him disappear down the road toward the
Great Wall and the expansive desert.

Who built this majestic ancient wonder? Who's watching over it? Who yearns for it? Who will one day come
back along the ancient road leading from it?

Will they meet again one day? When that time comes, she'll certainly be happier to see him stand before
her.
