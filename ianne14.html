LI XIN

Background

Born of royal blood, Li Xin has held on firmly to his belief to put his country first. However, because his
father was defeated in a power struggle, he bore the legacy of his father's hatred and wrath in his heart.
Swearing vengeance, he came to the Great Wall alone, obsessed with reclaiming his glory and territories.
As he has to choose between the forces of light and darkness, just where will his sword be pointed at?

Lore

Li Xin hails from Chang'an but has no place to call home. Chang'an was once his home. His father was
originally the crown prince, but after being implicated in a treasonous plot, his entire family was
persecuted. Bloodied and banished, Li Xin could only accept the dark power that his father had traded his
life for.

While he was alone and despondent, Li Xin was confronted by an alchemist, who spoke confusing yet
foreboding words, "You've lost Chang'an, and I've lost my most beloved possession. If you help me regain
the Great Wall, I'll help you get back your city." Li Xin agreed to trade the Great Wall for Chang'an. Following
the alchemist's instructions, he joined the alchemist's organization, the "Yaotian Society," and arrived at
the borderlands with a burning desire for revenge. He would have to perform well there in order to
eventually return to his true "home."

However, he detested the Great Wall. The isolation was maddening, but his pride and drive set him apart
from his peers. A few veteran soldiers took no notice of his aloofness and approached him to chat. They
told him that not long before, another man like him had volunteered to guard the Great Wall. He had been
strong, but somehow suspicious. That man had escaped after murdering his commanding officer, and it
was rumored that the traitor was still lingering nearby. Having finished the story, the veterans expected Li
Xin to react with shock or fear. Instead, he simply inspected his sheathed blade and replied flatly,
"Perfect. I'll give that traitor a taste of this."

Shortly after, a gang of thieves on horseback launched a surprise attack on the Great Wall. While the other
soldiers were gearing up for battle, Li Xin stood alone, gazing in the direction of Dunefort with a curious
expression on his face. He'd seen through the enemy's plotting. The thieves were only a diversion—their
true target was Dunefort! Li Xin immediately reported this conclusion to the commander, who sent out a
signal for reinforcements. Great Wall Guardians rode hard toward Dunefort. Li Xin searched the battlefield
for the enemy's leader, seeking to snatch up top honors in combat achievement. After all, that aim was
the only thing that made the isolation of the Great Wall endurable for him.

Just then, a woman with long red hair blocked his path, saving his life from the swift blade of the enemy
leader. Now outnumbered, the enemy turned to flee. His wicked silhouette scaled the wall, and he led his
troops in a hasty retreat. Noticing the guard badge on the woman's armor, Li Xin asked, "You... You're the
traitor?" The woman laughed, "Me? How could I be a traitor?" Li Xin stared in the direction the enemy
leader had fled and asked, "Who was that man, and what does he want with Dunefort?" "He's a king
without a kingdom," she replied. "A man without a homeland."

A king without a kingdom, a man without a homeland... Li Xin felt as if he had been struck by lightning.
Still dazed, he heard the woman softly singing, "The winds of war have enveloped our world... Where are
all the kings and lords?" The Great Wall Guardians had won a great victory, and Li Xin had earned his long-
desired merit, but there was not a sliver of joy in his heart. Recalling what the woman had said, he asked
his commander about the origins of the enemy leader. It turned out that Dunefort had been built atop the
ruins of an ancient city—the Golden City that was once Prince of Lanling's home. Unless the man could
recapture Dunefort, he would always be a king without a kingdom, a man without a homeland. "Though
worthy of sympathy..." the commander proclaimed, "How could we possibly give him what he wants? If
the Great Wall stands, we survive. If the Great Wall falls, we perish. And Dunefort is an integral outpost of
the Great Wall."

As the bonfire burned late into the night, Li Xin avoided the celebrating crowd and climbed atop a
watchtower to look out over the land. Two paths unfolded before his eyes. One path was golden and
glittering but led into the abyss. The other was rugged and perilous but led toward... Chang'an.
Chang'an... His real home—his true homeland. Here at the Great Wall, he would only ever be a foreigner in
a foreign land. "The route from Chang'an to the Great Wall... That is where my soul dwells..."
