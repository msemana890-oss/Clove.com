AUGRAN

Background

Qi's life should have been filled with light and joy. He was friendly, outgoing, and full of youth and
laughter. But then the Wilting happened, and a sudden disaster took all of his friends overnight. Qi carries
this pain in his heart as he marches on. With his spear, he cut himself off from the past and settled in the
marshes. Now, he is Augran, the ruthless High Priest who commands the power of life and death, and the
only one who can put an end to the Wilting.

Lore

As the High Priest, Augran is feared by all. But as Qi, he bears his mission alone.

When he was a child, Qi lived with his grandmother in the Forest Temple. Later, he set out to save Dyadia,
a girl who had been displaced by the Wilting. Since then, the two stuck together, and vowed to join the
Order of the Seers and protect the forest. But then the Wilting struck again, wiping out his grandma's
Forest Temple.

After that, he was taken in by his master. Working hard to join the Order of the Seers, he carried his and
Dyadia's oath with him.

With every strike of his spear, every incantation, and every spirit returned to its eternal resting place,
another piece of that cheerful Qi disappeared.

Every day when returning home, he developed the habit of bowing his head and gazing into the Pool of
Shadows as he walked by, watching the bright reflections disappear one by one...

The Pool of Shadows will show you the silhouettes of the people you love. His master told him that once
there was none left, he was ready to become the High Priest.

A High Priest cannot afford to have emotions.

That day finally came when his master, who had always preached the importance of not giving in to
emotion, was so overcome with his obsessions that he transformed into an evil spirit.

Full of anguish, Qi banished his master's spirit with his spear. He was now destined to be Augran the High
Priest, with no path back to his former self.

When Qi opened his eyes, his pupils had transformed, heaven and earth had changed, the pulse of the
world was still, and his burning heart had run cold.

Tragedies without end had plagued this land. Now, he would be the one to end them. The Wilting, the
broken Sky Dome, all would end when the Nine Seers patched up the sky.

He stepped out of the Marsh of Amnesia. The world lay roiling and clamoring before him.

Qi is dead. I am the Augran, High Priest of the Seers.
