HUANG,ZHONG

Background

This old veteran may have had thoughts about retiring, but his early ambitions never went away, and he
has held onto his principle of protecting others. In the pursuit of more power, his army gave into the
Blood Clan's temptations, causing Huang Zhong to leave in a fit of disdain. Fate then led him to Liu Bei
and his gang, who helped him give his machine gun an upgrade. On the battlefields of the Red Cliffs, the
old soldier fought a bloody battle and regained the fighting spirit he once displayed in his youth.

Lore

The veteran once thought it was time for him to retire.

The setting sun illuminated the ruined walls and his old, scarred body. He'd worked hard his entire life,
but was left with nothing to show for it: no money, no family, no home, no comrades...

Human beings must learn to accept old age. What alternative was there? Even his trusty cannon had
submitted to the rust.

He had not once felt fear when fighting the Feralis, and had confidently held his own against thousands
of troops. But... sorcery? Dark arts? Many of his companions had accepted it for greater power. After all,
even their leader had succumbed.

Sorcery!

He alone scorned and refused to acknowledge it.

"Stubborn old man." "You've given up on yourself." "He's got one foot in the grave anyway, it's no surprise
he's unwilling to change."

Sarcasm, ridicule... Such voices surrounded him. This world is getting incomprehensible...

Power?

Hmph! A monster with power is still a monster.

And people are people, humanity and all.

He was born a human, and he would die as one.

He left decisively. He only wanted to live out his fin阿al chapter in the most fulfilling way an old veteran
could.

So, he accepted this impossible task.

Fighting alongside a supposed descendant of nobility, a broadsword-wielding Ferali hunter, and a... half-
ferali mechsmith... was a strange feeling. He had been battling Ferali for most of his life, and he never
expected to end up fighting side by side with one of them. The mechsmith helped wipe the rust from his
cannon, and then busied himself with modifying it under the guidance of the young man holding a
strange fan.

His jaw dropped in amazement. The old bronze barrel shone brighter than gold, and rotating bearings
allowed the entire chassis to change shape.

Veterans do not die; rather, they are reborn. Refined.

It felt as though his blood, hot in his veins, began to course faster.

This ragtag squad set out to accomplish a solemn and almost impossible mission: to save the Shu
territory. Their powerful enemy was the legion that the veteran had just left, made almost invincible by
sorcery.

But when the ancient Wonder was lit up and the Red Cliffs burned, these weak-willed monsters fell in
droves, while the old soldier and his cannon stood strong like an immovable mountain. He was older than
any other soldier on the battlefield, but he had never been so youthful and full of vigor.

Humans may lose battles, but they will never lose the war.

Especially veterans.

"Time to make an explosive entrance!"
