CHARLOTTE

Background

Elegant and beautiful, Charlotte is a knight of noble heritage from Sunset. Her ancestors came from
across the sea, and after several generations of business dealings, her family established its reputation
within Sunset. Now, a series of tragic events have stirred up chaos in the city. To defend her family's pride
and honor, Charlotte has decided to follow the trail to Kyokutou and hopefully find the source behind all
this chaos.

Lore

Charlotte is the most capable of the heirs to a noble family in Sunset.

Her ancestors carved out their territory from the treacherous waves with their extreme courage and
persistence, and accumulated glory for generations through countless conflicts against other cities and
battles against pirates.

She achieved one victory after another in duels between nobles and left red roses to the defeated as a
gift.

But misfortune struck the family one day. Ships that received orders to sail eastward kept sinking around
the Tonpuu Shore, and these incidents triggered unrest in the city, as the families of the missing crew
begged for explanations, and merchants that suffered huge losses banded together to demand
compensation. Many knights, who came to serve the family because they admired the family's reputation,
chose to depart.

The pirates also assembled and launched an attack on the family, who failed to defend themselves. The
pirates took over the villas, and the remaining family members decided to pay a ransom to salvage what
little glory they have left.

Only Charlotte refused, because to a noble, losing honor was worse than death.

Before they reached an agreement with the pirates, she paid a visit to the pirate ship with her sword.
After a full night of fighting, she defeated every single pirate and exiled them from the city.

But things didn't end there. No one knew why the ships sank, and the source of the unrest has not been
identified. She needed to bring closure to the grieving families and the dead sailors.

After sorting through the leads she had, she thought the answers she was looking for might be in
Kyokutou, a city to the east. With her sword and red roses by her side, and her duty to protect the family
reputation on her shoulder, she boarded a ship that took her to faraway places, just like her ancestors did.

The ancestors accumulated the honor they once had, and Charlotte will protect the family honor going
forward.
