LIU BEI

Background

As the lord of Shu, Liu Bei's most fateful decision properly begins with a meeting between the two lords
and future enemies, him and his old friend Cao Cao, a very different kind of ruler known for his tyranny
and calculating manipulation. It was Liu Bei's acknowledgment of the secret "Heavenly Scrolls" handed
into his safekeeping that initially sparked Liu Bei's great war.

Liu Bei's journey past the principality of the conniving Duke Du You found him the recipient of an
orphaned child due to his reputation for kindness and, unwilling to abandon him to the conflict, agreed to
take him into his care. The forces of Liu Bei were then intercepted by a group of Feralis who sought to
slay the child as well as an equally strong group of Ferali hunters opposed to the same. A battle ensued,
during which an enchanted box given along with the baby fell out of his swaddling. The box fell on the
ground and, breaking, released all the memories that had been locked within by some unknown sorcery
of Liu Bei's former life. He looked around at his companions with new eyes and assured in his conviction
of an important mission left unfinished in his last life.

Lore

"Many years ago, I walked past Kongming's hut many many times, always acting nonchalant. He always
passed me by without even so much as a glance, but on the 180th time, he turned to me and spoke thus:
'Today, you meet your destiny.'"

"He emphasized that he was not a fortune teller, then walked away gracefully. A real character."

"I made my way toward the commandery inspector's place. This is a shameful story. The Heavenly Scrolls
was a general term for any ancient texts that recorded secrets of mechcraft or sorcery. My ancestors
once ruled over a kingdom, and when it was lost, they handed over their Heavenly Scrolls to a loyal
minister for protection. It's a shame that I was so young and innocent. I made the mistake of trusting my
former sworn brother Cao Cao, and told him of the scrolls at a banquet over some wine. Soon after, Cao
Cao's army, who coveted the Heavenly Scrolls, invaded my homeland."

"The commandery inspector was the inside man then. To settle the matter with him, I began monitoring
his movements under the guise of selling straw sandals."

"But on that day, something seemed very wrong. There was thick smoke billowing up from the mansion.
Could it be a fire?"

"A crowd gathered in excitement, and amidst the chaos, an old man tore his shoes. I offered him a new
pair and was just about to ask him about the situation, when he became overwhelmed with emotion and
handed me a large package, before disappearing in an instant."

"A generous reward? No! It turned out to be a crying baby."

"A shadow fell and a guy holding a snake-like spear stared at me as if I... no, as if the child in my hands
was his lifelong nemesis."

"'Hand it to me.' There was a strange, terrifying color to his eyes. It was pure hatred."

"I hugged the child closely. 'What do you want with my son?'"

"He let out a desolate laugh. 'No. This is the grandson of my sworn enemy.'"

"'No, this is my son. His name is Liu Shan, nicknamed A Dou. What, you want to check our ID?' I never
knew I could embellish with such confidence."

"His eyes widened and he was about to say something, when a huge broadsword came crashing down
like a mountain, rending a safe distance between us."

"'You've nowhere to run, Ferali!' The huge man in green drew his broadsword and slashed again, but this
time it was blocked by the spear."

"Taking advantage of the situation, I quickly shouted: 'The soldiers are here!', and ran away immediately."

"The man with the snake spear was taken aback for a moment before giving chase, followed closely by
the man in green."

"The baby started crying loudly as we ran, and I felt a cool wetness on my hands. He had peed himself!
Damn! As I scrambled to undo its soaked clothing, something slipped out of his swaddle."

"It was... an iron box."

"The snake spear man's expression changed completely."

"Remembering something, I took out my ancestral key."

"It fitted perfectly, and the iron box opened. Light shone out from inside. Could it be... the Heavenly
Scrolls?"

"The three of us reached out our hands in unintended unison. As soon as we touched the box, fragments
of memories poured into our minds. Since ancient times, since even a past that transcends time, my
brothers lived and died with me. We were together through thick and thin, but they died before they could
fulfill their ambitions. I stood helpless as I watched them fall one by one before my eyes. Was this a
previous life? I hated it, I hated to part with them in this way."

"When I looked up again, our three pairs of eyes met, with tears streaming down our cheeks."

"Was this my encounter with destiny...?"

"I choked back tears and forced a smile: 'It seems we were destined to meet. Why not swear a
brotherhood?'"

"Together we live and die, through joy and sorrow!"

"Together through the good and the bad. Heaven and earth shall be our witness!"

"'No matter how things change as time passes, we shall share the fate of life and death.'"

"In a brotherhood, the most important thing is loyalty!"
