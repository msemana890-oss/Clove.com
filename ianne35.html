UKYO,TACHIBANA

Background

Loneliness often accompanies true greatness, and while Ukyo Tachibana is known for his superb Iaijutsu
and gentlemanly demeanor, he also suffers the ravaging effects of tuberculosis. His desire for love was
awakened by a chance encounter with a young girl, Kei Odagiri. Upon learning that his lover was
distressed by her mother's serious illness, he resolutely embarked to Kyokutou in search of the magical
flower rumored to cure all diseases.

Lore

In a village by the river dwelled Ukyo Tachibana, the descendant of a fallen samurai family. He was known
for his mastery over Iaijutsu, the quickdraw sword technique. His soft demeanor captivated the hearts of
young women, none of whom suspected that he was in fact very lonely and felt empty inside. This was
because he had suffered from a respiratory disease for many years, and he was convinced that he would
die alone.

One day, everything changed. To flee the pestilence of the city, a Lord took his family to the countryside,
and Ukyo Tachibana encountered Kei Odagiri, the equally quiet daughter of the Lord. Her soft words and
dignified aura stood out, but due to the differences in their social standings, they rarely saw each other.

Kei was trying to pick the blooming flowers on the cliff for her ill mother when their paths crossed. Ukyo
picked them for her, and Kei expressed her gratitude. "Do you like flowers, too? I was moved by their
beauty." Like tossing a stone into a quiet lake, her words sent ripples through Ukyo Tachibana's heart, and
he wanted to live more than ever, so he could pick all the flowers for the girl that he loved.

The pestilence was still spreading, and the villagers lived in fear. Kei was worried because her mother was
still sick, when news came that the flowers blooming in the capital could cure any sickness and extend
life. Looking at his frustrated lover, Ukyo Tachibana decided to go to the capital, which, in addition to
the plague, was also threatened by the zealous Blood Clan. But if the flowers there could bring her smile

back...

With Nameless, his hand-forged blade, Ukyo Tachibana left for the capital.
