HAN XIN

Background

Han Xin may seem compliant and cooperative, but beneath the surface, he is an unyielding strategist
filled with an ambition that even rivals that of his master, Liu Bang. He has endured countless
humiliations as a means to survive and achieve his goals, while silently nurturing the rage that has
imprinted itself upon his heart. Han Xin pretends to be just another one of Liu Bang's subordinates,
waiting for his chance to raise a storm in the Riverlands to avenge himself and return all the humiliation
he has suffered a hundred-fold.

Lore

Han Xin is by nature an uncompromising strategist.

Everyone in the Misty Marshes knew that Liu Bang, to whom Han Xin was loyal, would pay any price to get
what he wanted, and that he regarded his own interests as more important than anything else. However,
few people knew that Han Xin's ambition was on par with, if not greater than, Liu Bang's. No matter the
situation, Han Xin would always do that which benefited him.

For Han Xin, survival was difficult, but his will to live was strong. In order to survive and achieve his goals,
he chose to endure humiliation. This wasn't something he accepted reluctantly, it was a choice he made.

Therefore, when a young ruffian raised his sword to humiliate Han Xin, he chose to crawl pathetically
between the ruffian's legs. When an even more powerful leader appeared, he submitted and willingly
shackled himself.

He didn't just endure it blindly; he was biding his time until a single strike was all he needed.

Before he became a well-known man of power and brilliant strategist, Han Xin had been brewing up a
storm. A storm that would rip across the continent and inscribe his name in the annals of history.

He doesn't believe in destiny. He only believes in himself.

His long-awaited moment finally arrived. Liu Bang, who Han Xin was loyal to, was about to head into his
final battle with Xiang Yu, the Dark Master who was yet to awaken. Han Xin hatched an ingenious plan and
weaved a carefully constructed web.

He used an illusion to disguise himself as Consort Yu's fellow disciple, Liang, to get close to Consort Yu
He told her the cruel truth: that her sweetheart Xiang Yu was actually the Dark Master who would
threaten the entire continent, and the one that she had sworn to defeat. Han Xin took advantage of her
state of shock and bewitched her consciousness.

That night, when Xiang Yu was besieged by enemies and engaged in battle, Consort Yu walked out of the
enemy camp like a beautiful puppet, firing an arrow that pierced his heart. Han Xin had used Consort Yu
to defeat Xiang Yu.

In the darkness, Han Xin smiled. This was the moment he had been waiting for. One shot and he'd taken
victory.

He made a name for himself in this battle, and it spread across the continent like a thunderstorm. The
storm that he had been brewing has started.

"I will repay your damage hundredfold!"
