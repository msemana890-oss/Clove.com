WUKONG

Background

Rebellious by nature, Wukong hated being governed and restrained, and he openly despised the shackles
the hypocritical superbeings had placed on the Feralis. During those dark times, he led a rebellion.
Wherever his Golden Cudgel went, the shackles of oppression were shattered—and the Feralis freed. Until
the superbeings used their absolute power to seal him at the foot of Mountains, that is. But the fire within
him could not be extinguished, and now he awaits the one destined to forge a new path with him...

Lore

The Feralis are a species that existed on the continent before humans.

The arrival of the superbeings heralded the beginning of solar energy. Yearning for it, the feralis made a
pact with the superbeings that allowed the Feralis to maintain the Sun Tower for the superbeings. But in
the eyes of the superbeings, the feralis were nothing but uncivilized, beast-like creatures, and they made
them toil endlessly without ever fulfilling their promise.

This was a dark and cruel time for the Feralis.

Wukong is a Ferali, but he was stubborn, and untamed. While being oppressed, he realized the Feralis
were being taken advantage of, and he launched a rebellion, leading the Feralis in a bid for freedom.

Wukong swept across the continent like a golden hurricane. Wherever he went, he struck fear into the
hearts of humans and rulers while slaves cheered and shouted slogans of freedom.

The anxious rulers summoned a powerful Aorian in an attempt to subdue Wukong. However, Wukong just
laughed and waved his weapon, and where his Golden Cudgel struck, the shackles of oppression
shattered, freeing the Aorians. Those who were freed shouted Wukong's name and joined the ranks of the
rebellion.

Over time, more and more slaves joined Wukong's cause. His ranks grew so large that the humans
thought that the continental regime would be overturned.

This was the first Ferali rebellion in the history of the continent. However, the Ferali force was later
betrayed and the rebellion ended in failure.

Sealed under a mountain, Wukong and the rebellion came to an abrupt end. His name would only appear
in the literature of history scholars and children's bedtime stories, but his story continued to inspire those
who were oppressed to defy oppression and fight with courage.

Many years later, when the superbeings no longer ruled the continent, the Feralis were still being
discriminated against by most due to their unique appearance. Of course, there were also some who were
willing to accept them, thus the hybrid race continued.

One day, a monk passing by the foot of Jade Hill found Wukong sealed there. The monk called out to
Wukong and invited him to journey to the west together in search of the origins of the world and to find a
solution to the impending crisis.

"This crisis surpasses the limits of humans and Feralis, so come join me in search of the origins."

Even after several millennia, Wukong's spirit could not be crushed. Though he had failed before, he never
regretted it. He had fought with all his might and had nothing to show for it, but he stayed hopeful.

In response to the monk's calls and chants, the earth began to tremble violently.

Just like the time when he was born, Wukong once again broke through the shackles and was reborn.

"I'll stir up a storm like they've never seen!"
