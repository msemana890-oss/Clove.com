LUBAN NO.7

Background

Luban No.7 is a puppet created by Master Luban using advanced mechanical techniques. Though it may
look small and adorable, it is capable of causing massive destruction. During the invasion of the

Lore

Luban No.7 was the product of an argument between two geniuses. While constructing the Jixia Academy,
a craftsman called Mozi took the initiative to reconstruct machinery, the greatest ancient invention,
and became one of the Three Sages of Jixia. Luban, another master craftsman, considered this the
biggest regret of his life, and decided that Mozi would be his nemesis. From that point on, every
topic Luban chose to study ran opposite to Mozi's pacifism.

When Mozi finished the construction of the Academy and started working on Chang'an, Luban threw
Luban himself into the research of destructive machinery. However, both Jixia Academy and the
walls of Chang'an managed to withstand wave after wave of attacks from Luban's machines. The
embarrassment was unbearable! Luban coolly evaluated himself and his rival, and decided to build
something greater, and more destructive, to launch an all-out surprise attack on Mozi. He codenamed
his project "Luban" after himself. As his research progressed, the design went through several
iterations. Finally, Luban No.7 was born.

As Luban's greatest work, the Luban No.7 was a force to be reckoned with on the battlefield. His Shark
Cannon blew away everything in its path, and the Blowfish Airship summoned air support to blast
enemies with explosions. Not long after Luban No.7 was created, the Blood Clan launched an invasion.
Luban disliked Mozi, but he had to admit that Mozi's words made sense. Yong and Jixia depended on each
other, and Luban No.7 was needed in Yong. Mozi took Luban No.7 to assist with the battle in Yong, but he
somehow lost him along the way...

Luban No.7 wandered the land. Those who came across the puppet could hear his muttered yearning for
Luban, even if his language module had not been fully trained.

"My creator, Master Luban, IQ of 250. Worthy of respect!"
