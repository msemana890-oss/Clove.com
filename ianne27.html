GAO,CHANGGONG

Background

Originally a prince of the Golden City, he witnessed the death of his parents and the destruction of his
country, after which he went from a pampered prince to a bereaved wanderer without a home. However,
through harsh training, this beaten-down man became a fearsome assassin. In order to reclaim his
homeland from Dunefort, he marched on the Great Wall, fully aware that it was a vainglorious endeavor.

Lore

The Golden City was one of the earliest Alsahraa kingdoms to rise. After it conquered the other kingdoms
in a war, Alsahraa enjoyed a short-lived period of peace and prosperity. The kingdom eventually fell to the
Iron Riders from Chang'an, and both the king and the queen perished. The royal family led their people
elsewhere to establish another kingdom, while those from Chang'an took over the old capital and built
Dunefort.

Prince of Lanling was supposed to grow up to become a respectable ruler and carry on the legacies of the
previous kings. However, after witnessing his parents leap to their deaths from the spire as the troops
from Central Plains breached the city wall, and then seeing Dunefort built on the ruins of his beloved city,
the seed of hatred was sown in his heart. He decided to take back the lost land and exact revenge on
Dunefort as well as the Great Wall.

A mysterious hermit from the mountains took Prince of Lanling away and trained him to become an
assassin.

The once-noble prince became a killer in the shadows. He was hired by powerful people many times to
assassinate peace envoys, leading to clashes, suspicion, and fear.

The kingdom that was supposed to be his had become a wasteland, and the forgotten land was buried
under ruins. But Prince of Lanling never gave up on his revenge.

An impenetrable and colossal obstacle stood between him and his goal: the Great Wall. As long as it
stood, the eastern empire would not fall. To take back what was his, he must destroy Dunefort, which
stood above the old capital, as well as the Great Wall.

But since the Great Wall was under the watchful eyes of the Great Wall Guardians around the clock, they
had to be taken down first before the Wall could be breached.

The prince scaled the Great Wall repeatedly to discover its secrets. He learned every guardian's name and
hometown, the factions they belonged to, and the conflicts between the factions. He patiently searched
for weaknesses in their defense and sowed the seeds of discord at the right time, just as he did during his
assassinations.

Finally, the leader of the Great Wall Guardians fell, and Mulan, accused of being a traitor, fled. Suddenly
leaderless, the guardians were in disarray. The chaos only lasted a brief moment, but that was all the
prince needed. He notified the barbarians living in the desert, who had been eyeing the Wall for a long
time, and their timely attack turned the Great Wall into a bloody inferno.

One man against a kingdom. A ridiculous idea, yet the prince accomplished it with ease.

He was a cold-blooded and mysterious killer, a ghost feared by those on the Wall.

As Prince of Lanling repeated his maneuver, the number of dead grew steadily. There was fear in the air
as he played the guardians like fools—until one night, when a blade from the darkness stopped him in his
tracks.

"So you're the ghost on the Wall!" A scarlet figure emerged from the dark... It was Mulan, the woman who
was accused of being a traitor.

The prince smiled at his surprise enemy. He was getting bored with winning all the time, and Mulan would
make a good match.

Even if there were countless obstacles in his way, even if his goal seemed almost impossible, Prince of
Lanling, a king without a kingdom, will someday take back what once belonged to him.

"I draw my country's borders with my blade."
