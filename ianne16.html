MAYENE

Background

Mayene is the most troublesome student in Jixia. She is very talented, and clever, but also has rather
skewed ideas. She's capricious and spontaneous, and a constant source of distraction that Fuzi had to
keep an eye on. Due to her dissatisfaction with her hero father, she is convinced that all heroes are fools.
Her dream of teaching at Jixia and retiring there was cut short by her mother's dying wish, which was
that she go to Navenia to look for a captain named Red Hawk. She thought it would be an easy task, until
she met that "dumbass" of a mercenary...

Lore

Mayene is the most difficult student at the Jixia Martial Arts Academy. She's talented and smart but also
full of strange ideas. A free spirit, she's the "special" student Fuzi often has to keep an eye on.

Her mother left Navenia and fell in love at first sight with her father, a hero from Yong, and this
transcontinental love gave birth to Mayene. At the age when every child wished their father were a hero,
she regarded heroism as nothing but trouble. Being a hero came with expectations, and fulfilling those
expectations often meant making sacrifices. Heroes were no longer people but symbols. Idols. Life is full
of possibilities, and choosing to be a hero seemed like one of the worst decisions to her.

Her father was fulfilling his heroic duties when her mother passed away, vindicating her understanding of
heroes. She was entrusted to Fuzi, the headmaster of Jixia Academy. She had zero aptitudes in sorcery
but showed incredible martial arts talent. Mayene defeated the teacher evaluating her entrance exam in
the most unorthodox way possible, shocking the entire academy. After being enrolled, she started dozing
off during classes, only passing them with minimum effort. She diligently applied her talents to taking
time off and avoiding work. The teachers gave up on her, but Fuzi knew that she was a diamond in the
rough.

Fuzi didn't give up on her. After getting caned several times, she tried to figure out how to get him back
and shake him off for good. During her countless wrangles with Fuzi, she went from not being able to do
anything to putting up a good fight, and eventually, she was able to escape him entirely. There wasnothing
in Jixia that could hold her back! nothing in Jixia that could hold her back!

When she came of age, Fuzi shipped her to Navenia with a mechcraft luggage and threatened to kick her
out if she didn't fulfill her mother's final wish. She reluctantly accepted the mission, which she thought
was a walk in the park... Until she met a "fool" named Biron, and her destiny changed. When she was
thrown out of a window by a steel arm, she knew that fleeing was in her best interest. However, upon
seeing Biron in dire straits, she took the "troublesome" path for the first time in her life. Little did she
know this would be her first step toward becoming a "foolish" hero.

"Martial arts is never the same, and neither is life."
