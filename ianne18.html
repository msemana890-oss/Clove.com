NEZHA

Background

The powerful Nezha can be defeated, but never tamed. Due to an illness he had as a child, his master
Taiyi Zhenren replaced his heart with a Wonder Key. However, his overwhelming power alienated him from
his peers, and there save him, Zhenren gave him his own heart, making him a full person again.
Now, the boy reborn in flameswas never a moment when he felt free from the rage burning within him.
When the superbeings destroyed his homeland in the name of uprooting the Taorians, Nezha's fury ignited the
battlefield. But the exertion was too much, and by the end, Nezha lay dying among the ashes. In order to
save him, Zhenren gave him his own heart, making him a full person again. Now, the boy reborn in flames
will never yield to anyone.

Lore

Chentang Pass is a mountain pass on the Eastern Sea, around which a city has gradually formed. Nezha
grew up in Chentang Pass, where his father was a senior officer. He showed extraordinary talent from an
early age: his strength, speed, and combat prowess far surpassed his peers, and even his older brothers
were no match for him.

But Nezha had no friends. He found it hard to resist the urge to go berserk, which kept his peers at a
distance. He only had one person, his mentor, on his side.

However, the mentor hid the truth from him: Nezha's heart had been replaced by a Wonder Key, whose
power made Nezha extremely strong, but it also meant that he could feel no emotion besides anger.

The rulers of Celestia uncovered this secret and ordered Nezha's father to send him to Celestia as a
hostage, and the mentor volunteered to accompany him. Celestia seemed like a bustling place to Nezha,
but he also found it boring and stifling. Nezha couldn't help but annoy the people there, taking delight in
their anger and helplessness. It was the only pleasure in his caged existence.

The people couldn't do anything to Nezha, so they decided to take out their revenge on the master. They
reported him as a fugitive criminal and had him sent to jail. In a fit of rage at this injustice, Nezha escaped
from Celestia.

As punishment for escaping, the rulers demanded that Nezha's father completely eradicate the feralis
from the Eastern Sea. When Nezha returned to Chentang Pass, he found that storms and tidal waves had
swept across the entire city, and the innocent citizens had been slaughtered by the enraged feralis. His
hometown had been reduced to a vast expanse of water, having sacrificed itself to trap an enemy a
hundred times stronger than itself.

The rulers' aim was not actually to get rid of the feralis, but to make things difficult for Nezha's father and
make Chentang Pass and the feralis perish together!

Nezha was devoured by rage. He charged toward the surging Eastern Sea, fighting frantically against the
feralis until every last ounce of his energy was spent.

At the final moment, his master arrived from Celestia and gave him his own heart, activating a magic
circle. Inside the circle, the dying Nezha gained a new life. His injured body was reshaped by alchemy, but
ever since, he cannot sense touch nor pain, and his growth in stature completely halted. The one thing he
can feel is his heart beating in his chest—a human heart.

Looking out over the calm sea, Nezha suddenly began to experience emotions other than anger: sadness,
longing, guilt, gratitude... He had never felt such things before. He was finally a real "human" for the first
time.

By some miracle, the mentor survived. He apologized to Nezha, telling him the truth about the Wonder
Key, but Nezha didn't want to go into any of that. From now on, he must choose his own path, protecting
what he wants to protect and changing what he wants to change.

"My existence is one that defies order and reason!"
