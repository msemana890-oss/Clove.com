LAM

Background

The man who now goes by the code name Lam was exiled by his people for his inauspicious birth and
spurned by his peers for possessing unnatural abilities. He was left to die in the rubble of a battlefield but
was found by Cao Cao, who took him in and trained him as one of his covert assassins. Lam embraced the
lonely life of a hunter in the darkness, if only to survive yet another day. That would all change when he
was sent on a mission to eliminate Little Lute, a girl who appeared to him so utterly warm-hearted and
kind that shattered his beliefs and led him to abandon his past once and for all. Now, the ruthless
assassin is only willing to get his hands dirty for those he deems worthy.

Lore

The young man codenamed "Lam" was mercilessly exiled from his clan because they believed him to be
an "inauspicious person" with the power of calamity hidden in his body. Lam's happiest days were those
spent growing up with a group of orphans in a small fishing village next to the sea. When the flames of
war extended there, Lam unexpectedly aroused the dormant "shark power" in his body and killed the vile
enemy, protecting his friends.

The people were safe, but they were afraid of Lam's powers. Since then, the villagers regarded Lam as a
monster, and no one dared to approach him. Whenever young Lam tried to use his powers to protect
himself or others, he was met with frightened looks of rejection from everyone. Over time, Lam became
accustomed to being away from the crowd and living alone.

In the war-torn Three Kingdoms, corpses lined the scorched battlefields. Lam's only means of survival
was to rely on the fearsome power that made him an outcast to begin with. This orphan who survived the
purgatory-like battlefields grew up and was adopted by Cao Cao, the Lord of Wei. There were many others
like Lam in the Wei camp, whom Cao Cao had instructed should be taught the many combat arts, with the
intention of developing a bevy of capable generals for his use.

In order to survive, Lam, who had finally found a place to live, was forced to shut his heart and numbly
accept training. With his remarkable capabilities, Lam was naturally noticed by Cao Cao and given more
tasks. All he could do to lessen the suffering of his opponents during missions was to wield his blade with
swiftness and precision. Although he had long ceased to be that scared boy huddled up and trembling in
a corner, and had gained calmness and courage—he had also lost all emotion.

He completed his missions coldly, never socializing with others. With his terrifyingly lethal shark power,
he became a top killer amongst Cao Cao's men, an assassin who never missed his target. But no one
knew about the person hiding behind his unfeeling and nimble appearance, and his sharp and tenacious
blade.

This continued until Cao Cao assigned him to pursue the Jade Seal. Lam was wounded on the mission
and stranded on a deserted island until he was rescued by his target, Little Lute. Stranded together on
the island for many days, Lam was moved by her unguarded warmth and innocence. After having been
submerged in darkness for years, he suddenly saw a ray of light, and his long-numb heart was redeemed.

Cao Cao and his retinue arrived at the deserted island accompanied by Sima Yi, the Wei strategist, ready
to cross blades with Little Lute. Finally choosing to act according to his own will, Lam put his life on the
line to provide cover for Little Lute to escape. The cold killer who had been through unspeakable things
turned his back on his mission and past, abandoned Cao Cao and the land of Wei, and finally became
himself again, setting out on a journey of his own. "My past lies buried... I reach for the future."
